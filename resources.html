Resources

Local supports for life in Los Angeles & Orange County.

A gently curated collection of hotlines, county services, community organizations, and tools — tailored for residents of LA County and Orange County. Offered as companions to your care, not replacements for professional support when you need it.

Hotlines

If you need immediate support.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988

Free, confidential, 24/7 support for anyone in crisis or emotional distress. Available in English, Spanish, and many other languages.

National · 24/7

LA County ACCESS Center

1-800-854-7771

LA County Department of Mental Health's 24/7 help line for mental health services, crisis response, and referrals across the county.

LA County · 24/7

OC Health Care Agency Crisis Line

1-855-625-2570

Orange County's 24/7 crisis assessment and mental health support line, with mobile crisis response teams available.

Orange County · 24/7

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Connect with a trained crisis counselor by text, any time of day or night.

Text · 24/7

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-7233

Free, confidential 24/7 support for anyone experiencing intimate partner violence, with warm referrals to local LA and OC shelters.

DV · 24/7

2-1-1 (LA & OC)

Dial 211

Free 24/7 connection to housing, food, health, mental health, and social services across LA and Orange County.

LA & OC · 24/7
